In the following screens, an interactive journal entry will appear on the right hand
side of the screen. There are highlighted phrases in the text, and if you hover your mouse
over it, the image on the left will change. There are also blanks in the text, and if you
click the blank, options will appear on the left. Click on an option to replace the blank
in the text with that option. The options you chose determine the outcome of the story.
