Entry 4: Acceptance/Resolve

Been a while since my last entry. Just need to spill my thoughts here before I make my decision. I really like this girl. No way to avoid that. We’ve been hanging out nearly every day, sometimes working on our journals, other times just being together as friends. And every day, I have to ask myself if it’s the right time to ask her out. I’m sure she’s interested in me...but I keep second-guessing myself. It’s tough, I’m sure she’s interested, but I’m a little scared of losing what we’ve got. We hang out constantly, we message each other late at night, we’re just comfortable around each other, no awkward silences at all... Actually, what am I doing? This is it! I like her, she ___________ likes me back, what am I waiting for? No, I’m sick of this anxious back-and-forth between me and myself, I’m asking her out tomorrow. Someone’s gotta confess first, and it might as well be me. Tomorrow, after club for sure.
